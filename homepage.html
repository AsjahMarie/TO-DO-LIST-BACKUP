How CheckMate works!

Navigate to the Task List page!

In the bottom right corner you will find a circle with a "+" on it — this is where you add your tasks! You'll be prompted to give a task name, due date, and your preferred color. You will also have the ability to add a subtask, and share this task — or tasks — with a friend! Entering their email will display your default mailing app with a pre-filled email to send to your friend! (Don't forget to add your task!)
Made a mistake? Not to worry! click on the "edit task" button and fix any errors you may have made!
Finished your task? Way to go! Click on the "complete & remove button" to erase your task — but be warned, when you delete a task it's gone forever!
Want to start from scratch? Click on the button in the bottom left corner

Click on the calendar tab!

Here you will see a calendar! Click on any day to see if you have a task that is due on that day!
Remember, you can only add tasks from the task list page!
